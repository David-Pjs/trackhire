# TrackHire — Product Context

## Product Purpose
TrackHire is a job application tracker built for active job seekers. It gives them a visual Kanban pipeline — Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, Rejected — so they always know where every application stands. No spreadsheets, no lost follow-ups.

## Users
Active job seekers managing 10–50+ concurrent applications. Frustrated by spreadsheets. Technically comfortable. Want clarity and control, not motivation. They use this daily, often anxiously — they're in the middle of a job search.

## Register
brand (landing page is the product surface being designed)

## Brand Tone
Utilitarian confidence. The tool does one thing and does it well. No cheerleading, no aspirational fluff. Copy is short, direct, and factual — like the Acctual reference.

## Aesthetic
Dark, warm-neutral palette (obsidian base with stone undertone). Orange accent (#F97316) used scarcely. The app itself is a premium dark tool — the landing should feel like its entrance, not a marketing pitch.

## Anti-references
- Centered hero with big headline + subtext + single CTA button (generic SaaS)
- Purple/blue gradients
- Glassmorphism cards
- "Trusted by..." social proof rows
- Floating blob backgrounds
- Stats grids ("12+ companies / $2M raised")
- Gradient text

## Font
Geist Sans — geometric, clean, slightly technical
